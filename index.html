27 January 2026

Creative Cover for Trafficanti d’Arte

Trafficanti d’Arte is a tattoo studio and ink & apparel brand based in Milan, Via Mercantini 15. Led by internationally awarded tattoo artist Marco Galdo, a pioneer of ornamental, geometric, symmetrical and dotwork tattooing in Italy, the studio approaches the body as a true living canvas.

Alongside him, Stefania Salis creates beautiful garments entirely by hand, shaping fabric with the same care and intention that Marco shapes bodies with ink. Each piece reflects a shared vision: art that dresses the body, whether through tattoo or clothing.

Two crafts. One language. Art that lives on the body.

20 January 2026

Studio Atmosphere & Creative Process

Inside the studio, every project begins with dialogue. Each design is carefully developed to reflect personal identity, symbolism, and harmony with the body’s natural structure.

The creative process blends geometry, symmetry and precision. Every line is intentional. Every dot carries rhythm.

More than a tattoo session, it becomes a collaborative artistic ritual.

19 January 2026

Handcrafted Apparel Collection

The apparel collection reflects the same philosophy as the tattoo work: precision, balance, and intention.

Each garment is handmade, carefully constructed to highlight form and movement. Fabrics are selected with attention to texture and durability.

A wearable extension of the studio’s artistic universe.
